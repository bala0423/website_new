Imagine a future where all the world′s financial institutions are not only transparent, but operating on a open pre-defined rules system that is enforced based on truth. The moment someone disavowing or trying to cheat, the whole world would see. The biggest key to turning blockchain′s potential into reality is a collaborative effort among enterprises to create the network that used by everyone owned or controlled by no-one. The promise of cryptofinance is one of trustworthy financial systems. We can build smart contracts over the blockchain backbone that is an ubiquitous distributed ledger system that can transform the way we make transactions.
